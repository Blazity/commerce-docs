---
title: "Reviews Summary"
description: "Setup reviews summary for better user experience"
icon: "star"
---

This feature is optional and can be enabled by setting the `OPEN_AI_API_KEY`.

The review summary is generated based on all ratings, highlighting both the best and worst aspects of the product.

Summaries are updated weekly, contingent upon receiving new reviews. This approach ensures that SEO can effectively index changes while providing relevant and updated information to users.

To view the source, please check api/reviews/ai-summary/route.ts. You may adjust the prompt as needed.
